About India Digital Coin (IDC)

India Digital Coin (IDC) is a visionary digital currency designed for the youth and future traders of India. Built on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC), IDC aims to provide an affordable and accessible way to invest, trade, and grow in the digital economy. Our mission is to make digital assets a part of every Indian’s financial journey — from college desks to crypto decks.
