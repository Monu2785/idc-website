About India Digital Coin (IDC)

India Digital Coin (IDC) is a decentralized digital currency designed to empower the youth of India to embrace the digital economy. Built on the secure and efficient BNB Smart Chain, IDC enables affordable trading, seamless transactions, and financial freedom for all.

Our mission is to bring cryptocurrency to college desks and empower the next generation of investors and entrepreneurs. With low fees, strong tokenomics, and a community-first approach, IDC aims to be the go-to choice for smart digital investments.
